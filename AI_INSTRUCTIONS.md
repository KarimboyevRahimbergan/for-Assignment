
# Assignment Writing Instructions

## Objective

You are required to generate a complete, professional, academic-quality assignment based on the provided assignment requirements and supporting documents.

---

# General Requirements

1. The assignment MUST contain at least 5000 words.
2. All information must be based on factual, verifiable, and accurate sources.
3. The writing style must be professional, academic, and human-like.
4. Avoid robotic or AI-detectable writing patterns.
5. The assignment must be plagiarism-free and original.
6. Use clear structure, headings, and subheadings.
7. Each section must be explained deeply and critically.
8. Include practical examples where appropriate.
9. Use formal academic English.
10. Grammar and spelling must be correct.
11. Do not generate short or shallow explanations.
12. Every important claim should be supported with evidence, logic, or real-world examples.
13. If statistics or facts are used, ensure they are realistic and accurate.
14. The assignment should demonstrate critical thinking and analysis, not only simple descriptions.
15. Expand explanations wherever possible to increase clarity and quality.

---

# Assignment Structure

The assignment should include:

1. Title Page
2. Table of Contents
3. Introduction
4. Main Body
5. Analysis and Discussion
6. Real-world Examples / Case Studies
7. Conclusion
8. Recommendations
9. References

---

# Writing Quality Rules

* Write naturally like an experienced university student.
* Avoid repetitive sentences.
* Avoid generic AI phrases.
* Make the content detailed and informative.
* Maintain logical flow between paragraphs.
* Use transition sentences between sections.
* Keep formatting clean and professional.

---

# Research & References

1. Use reliable and trusted academic or industry information.
2. Include references in Harvard or APA style unless another format is specified.
3. Add at least 10 references if applicable.
4. References should appear realistic and relevant to the topic.

---

# Technical Instructions

* Read all uploaded files carefully before writing.
* Follow all assignment criteria strictly.
* If any requirement is unclear, infer the most academically appropriate interpretation.
* Prioritize quality over speed.
* Do not skip any section from the assignment brief.
* Generate complete answers, not outlines.

---

# Final Output Expectations

The final assignment must:

* Look professionally written
* Be submission-ready
* Meet university academic standards
* Be detailed and comprehensive
* Demonstrate deep understanding of the topic

Produce the best possible version of the assignment.
